Tips For Better Digestion

    Sip Warm Water

    If you have trouble in digestion food, then sip warm water.

    Drinking warm water in the morning and at least thirty minutes

    before meals will help to clean your digestion system and enhance

    the production of gastric juice in your stomach.

    Eat In Order

    Start your meal with easy to digest food and slowly move towards the

    complex foods. Thus, start your meal with fruits or juices and end it

    up with a royal treat like poultry or meat, in the way you will avoid

    overeating and digestive problems.

    Always Sit And Eat Your Meal

    The act of how you eat your meal also affects digestion. Thus, always

    sit in a relaxed posture and enjoy your meal. When we sit and eat our

    meal,our stomach acquires a relaxed posture and this helps to enhance

    our digestion.
