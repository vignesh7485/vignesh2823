Daily Fitness Tips.

  1. Walk every day.


  2. Drink plenty of water and healthy fluids.


  3. Never skip meals.


  4. Eat more of fruits and vegetables.


  5. Avoid deeply fried and spicy foods.


  6. Include more of alkaline and organic foods and avoid eating acidic foods.


  7. Munch on to nuts and seeds.


  8. Aim to eat at least 4-5 servings of fruits and vegetables daily.


  9. Eat a combination of different food groups(balanced diet).


  10. Avoid junk and street foods.


  11. Don't do overeating


  12. Curb your food cravings.


  13. Don't compare your body type with others.
